About Us

At Planted Foods Express we take pride in providing alternatives for dining outside of the home. Founder Orlando Adams was tired of having to pick between a salad and fries (that were likely cooked in oil along side items like chicken), or the Impossible burger (also likely cooked right along with regular meat items) at restaurants. He decided to take some of the things he prepares at home, marry them with items he would like to see when he goes out with his family, and bring them to the community. The plan was to give everyone, vegans/vegetarians/meat eaters too, "Plant based alternatives to the daily 'grind'" Orlando started his journey about 7 years ago when he decided to finally stop eating animal based meat in general. His thought was that everything you need comes from plants, lets not cause undo stress on the animals just to mass produce food for the sake of food. Think about it, that cow can't produce the same aminos you can't, so it gets them from somewhere. What are they fed beyond their mother's milk? Plants! This moved quickly to dairy, but admittedly letting go of cheese was tough. He did it though, and has some pretty tasty alternatives. We aren't trying to convert folks, but rather provide healthy, tasty alternatives that full on vegans and those meat eaters that want to have a "meatless Monday" or something like that, will enjoy!

Owner - Orlando Adams
